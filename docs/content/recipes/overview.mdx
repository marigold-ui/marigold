Recipes are specific combinations of design system components, tailored for specific use cases within a product. These compositions ensure consistency and cohesion across the product experience, while addressing scenarios that require more flexibility than a standard, agnostic design system component offers.

If you've created or discovered a useful recipe, feel free to share it with us. We'd love to review and include it in our collection to benefit the broader community.
