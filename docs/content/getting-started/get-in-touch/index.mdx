---
title: Get in touch
description: How to reach us when you need help with Marigold.
---

If you're stuck or have a question, we're here to help. Before reaching out, it's worth checking the documentation first. You might already find what you need.

If not, reach us through the [#design-system Slack channel (internal only)](https://reservix.slack.com/archives/C02727BNZ3J) or the [Support Board (internal only)](https://reservix.atlassian.net/servicedesk/customer/portal/77).

The Slack channel is the best starting point for all kinds of questions, whether it's something quick like "Where can I find the [UI kit](https://www.figma.com/design/NbTUW9zk15nN8idlfsEttS/%F0%9F%8C%BC-Marigold-CORE?node-id=1618%3A14089&t=XeVjosBqiOPFslb9-1)?" or something bigger like reviewing an implementation or talking through a complex use case. People regularly share their experiences and solutions there, and we want to keep that going.

If you need more time and context, feel free to ask for a pairing session or a meeting. Whether you're unsure about your implementation, how to use a Marigold component, or have a frontend question in general, we're happy to look at your code, review your approach, or just talk it through. If you're new to Marigold and want an introduction, let us know and we'll set up a walkthrough.

<Callout title="Want to contribute?">
  If you want to report a bug, request a feature, or contribute to Marigold,
  head over to [How to Contribute](/getting-started/how-to-contribute).
</Callout>
