---
title: Get in touch
description: How to reach us when you need help with Marigold.
---

If you're stuck or have a question, we're here to help. Before reaching out, it's worth checking the documentation first. You might already find what you need.

If not, you can reach the design system team through these channels:

- [Slack Channel (internal only)](https://reservix.slack.com/archives/C02727BNZ3J)
- [Support Board (internal only)](https://reservix.atlassian.net/servicedesk/customer/portal/77)

For quick questions like "Where can I find the [UI kit](https://www.figma.com/design/NbTUW9zk15nN8idlfsEttS/%F0%9F%8C%BC-Marigold-CORE?node-id=1618%3A14089&t=XeVjosBqiOPFslb9-1)?" or "What does this prop do?", the [#design-system Slack channel (internal only)](https://reservix.slack.com/archives/C02727BNZ3J) is the best place. People regularly share their experiences and solutions there, and we want to keep that going.

For anything that needs more time and context, like reviewing an implementation or talking through a complex use case, feel free to ask for a pairing session or a meeting in the Slack channel. Whether you're unsure about your implementation, how to use a Marigold component, or a frontend question in general, we're happy to look at your code, review your approach, or just talk it through.

If you're new to Marigold and want an introduction, post in [#design-system (internal only)](https://reservix.slack.com/archives/C02727BNZ3J) and we'll set up a meeting to walk you through the setup.

<Callout title="Want to contribute?">
  If you want to report a bug, request a feature, or contribute to Marigold,
  head over to [How to Contribute](/getting-started/how-to-contribute).
</Callout>
